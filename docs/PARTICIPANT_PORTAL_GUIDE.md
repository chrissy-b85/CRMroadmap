## 1. Accessing the Portal

### 1.1 Your invitation

When your support coordinator creates your account in the CRM, you will receive
an email invitation to activate your portal access. The email will come from
`notifications@your-org.com.au`.

Click **Activate my account** in the email and follow the prompts to set a
password. You will need to complete this step within 7 days; if the link has
expired, ask your support coordinator to resend the invitation.

### 1.2 Logging in

1. Go to `https://ndis-crm.your-org.com.au/portal`.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Click **Log in**.

If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot password** and follow the
instructions sent to your email.

### 1.3 Your portal home page

After logging in you will see:
- A summary of your current NDIS plan budgets
- Any invoices waiting for your approval
- Recent activity on your account

## 2. Understanding Your Budget

### 2.1 Budget categories

Your NDIS plan funding is divided into support categories. The most common are:

| Category | What it covers |
|----------|----------------|
| **Core Supports** | Daily activities, transport, consumables, social participation |
| **Capacity Building** | Improving your skills and independence |
| **Capital Supports** | Assistive technology and home modifications |

### 2.2 Reading your budget

Each category shows:
- **Total budget** — the amount in your plan for this category
- **Amount spent** — invoices that have been approved and processed
- **Remaining** — how much is left

A colour indicator shows how much of your budget has been used:
- 🟢 Green — plenty of budget remaining
- 🟡 Yellow — 80% or more has been used; consider discussing with your
  support coordinator
- 🔴 Red — budget is fully used

### 2.3 Budget alerts

You will receive a notification in the portal (and optionally by email) when
your budget in any category reaches 80% or 100%.

## 3. Reviewing and Approving Invoices

### 3.1 When do I need to approve an invoice?

Some invoices from your service providers require your approval before they are
processed and paid. When an invoice needs your approval, you will see a
notification on your portal home page and receive an email.

### 3.2 Reviewing an invoice

1. Click **Invoices** in the navigation menu or click the notification.
2. Click the invoice you want to review.
3. The portal shows:
   - The service provider's name
   - The date of the service
   - A description of the supports delivered
   - The amount charged
   - A link to download the original invoice PDF

### 3.3 Approving an invoice

If the invoice looks correct:
1. Click **Approve**.
2. A confirmation message appears. The invoice will now be processed.

### 3.4 Querying an invoice

If something doesn't look right:
1. Click **Raise a query**.
2. Type a message explaining your concern.
3. Click **Submit query**.

Your support coordinator will be notified and will follow up with the service
provider on your behalf.

## 4. Installing the App on Your Phone

The participant portal is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can add it to your
phone's home screen for quick access, just like a regular app.

### iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1. Open Safari and go to `https://ndis-crm.your-org.com.au/portal`.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Give the app a name (or keep the default) and tap **Add**.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open Chrome and go to `https://ndis-crm.your-org.com.au/portal`.
2. Tap the **⋮** (three-dot menu) in the top right.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**.
4. Follow the prompts.

Once installed, the portal icon will appear on your home screen and the app
will work offline for basic tasks.
